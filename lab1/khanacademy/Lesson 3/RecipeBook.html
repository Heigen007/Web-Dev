Classic Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Pancetta or bacon 150g
Parmesan cheese 50g, grated
Eggs 3 large
Garlic clove 1, peeled
Olive oil 2 tbsp
Black pepper To taste

Preparation Steps:

  1. Cook spaghetti in a large pot of boiling salted water, stirring occasionally, until al dente.
  2. Meanwhile, heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat, add pancetta, and cook until crisp, 8â€“10 minutes.
  3. Beat eggs and Parmesan in a bowl, then add a ladle of pasta cooking water to keep eggs from scrambling.
  4. Add pasta to skillet with pancetta and garlic, then mix in egg mixture. Remove from heat, add black pepper, and toss.
